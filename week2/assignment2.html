Critique of Panic.com


This website offers a nice sleek look. With the awesome links to their well develop apps. The seamless growth of the buttons is eye catching and graps attenion. With their logo right at the center of the page it forces people to remember that iconic image instead of just the name "Panic".

Their site also lacks in some basic points. One, they do not ahve a basic colo scheme going through their entire site, so I can't tell if its there blue logo or the random red and purple stripes are the main theme. Second is the homepage is spread vertically down and the first thing a normal window size sees is split images. Also, the big links to their blog and t-shirts don't change when hovering over them. THis lets people go right over top of them without realizing they are links to other sites. And lastly, the dropbox at the bottom has too light of text that it borders on illegible.
