I think the outline of this website is a great representation of how to sort through content. They have a nice flow of traffic to the main pages to their site and they reidderate the links throughout the page. They use multiple navigation lists to connect to the different pages, these list remain in the header and footer over all pages. When surfing through their site, the reader has a clear sense of what page they are on and how to navigate back to the home page.

Some down falls of their card sorting would be the unused space in their header and footer. Also the fact that not all their content can fit on to a normal window size. I would have spread the size out a bit farther knowing most people use a widescreen montior now. This woudl allow for more to fit verticaaly. And they should have thought about the order of the links to the social websites. These are reversed in the footer compared to the header.
